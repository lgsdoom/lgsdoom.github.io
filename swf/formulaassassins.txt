--------
ENGLISH:
--------
Your car has some technical problems.
The accelerator and the brake no longer respond to your commands.
To complete the race you can only use the steering wheel, your skills and your brand new sniper rifle.
Z to turn right and X to turn left.
MOUSE CLICK to shoot the enemies.
Good luck, assassin!
Tips & help: info (at) video-giochi.org

HOW TO PLAY:
MOUSE= fire
Z= turn right
X= turn left
